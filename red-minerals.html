Industrial Impact Analysis

The Iron Oxide Backbone

Red minerals, specifically Hematite, remain the cornerstone of global steel production. In 2025, the focus has shifted toward high-purity red ores for green steel smelting technologies.

Additionally, Vanadinite is becoming critical for the production of Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries, providing a safe alternative for large-scale energy storage.

Mineral Criticality Registry

Hematite

The primary iron ore. Essential for heavy infrastructure and the automotive industry.

Vanadinite

A secondary ore of Vanadium. Used in high-strength steel alloys and aerospace components.

The main source of mercury. Highly regulated and used specifically in precision instrumentation.
