Industrial Shielding Analysis

The High-Density Frontier

Gray minerals like Galena are the primary source of lead, which remains indispensable for radiation shielding in medical and nuclear facilities.

In the aerospace sector, Wolframite (Tungsten) is prized for having the highest melting point of all metals, making it critical for rocket engine nozzles and high-velocity kinetic penetrators.

Mineral Criticality Registry

Galena

The main ore of lead. Essential for battery tech and protecting sensitive electronics from cosmic radiation.

Chemistry: PbS

Wolframite

Source of Tungsten. Highly strategic mineral used in heavy-duty cutting tools and armor plating.

Chemistry: (Fe,Mn)WO4

Stibnite

Primary source of Antimony. Used to harden lead alloys and as a flame retardant in high-temp plastics.

Chemistry: Sb2S3
