🟨 Glowing Yellow Minerals! 🟨

Not everything that glitters is gold! Let's explore some amazing yellow rocks and metals found in the Earth.

  • Gold A shiny yellow metal and mineral that is highly valued all over the world for its beauty. Because it is so soft and rare, it is perfect for making jewelry!
  • Pyrite A metallic yellow mineral that is famous for tricking people! It looks so much like real gold that it earned the nickname "Fool's Gold."
  • Chalcopyrite Another tricky yellow mineral! It shares a metallic yellow color with pyrite and is actually a great place for miners to find copper.
  • Uranocircite A very cool mineral that glows yellow-green under special lights.
    🧪 Science Note: This mineral is radioactive, which means scientists have to wear special safety gear and use special tools just to study it!
