Sulfuric Acid: The Industrial Index

Native Sulfur remains the most important yellow mineral for heavy industry. Its role in producing sulfuric acid is considered the primary indicator of a nation's industrial strength.

In the energy sector, Carnotite serves as a key ore of uranium and vanadium, bridging the gap between nuclear energy production and high-capacity battery storage.

Mineral Criticality Registry

Native Sulfur

Fundamental for the chemical industry. Used in vulcanizing rubber, detergents, and phosphate fertilizers.

Chemistry: S8

Carnotite

A secondary mineral of vanadium and uranium. Essential for high-density energy and aerospace alloys.

Chemistry: K2(UO2)2(VO4)2·3H2O

Heliodor

Yellow Beryl. While prized for aesthetics, it is a source of Beryllium for aerospace and defense electronics.

Chemistry: Be3Al2Si6O18
