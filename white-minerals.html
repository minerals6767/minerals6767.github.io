The Quartz Bottleneck

In 2025, over 85% of global high-purity white quartz is channeled into the semiconductor and solar PV industries. Its piezoelectric properties remain irreplaceable for precision timing in quantum computing.

Furthermore, Titanium Dioxide (Rutile) continues to be the world's primary white pigment, essential for the thermal regulation of aerospace hulls and high-performance industrial coatings.
