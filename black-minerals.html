Strategic Importance

The Energy Catalyst

Black minerals like Graphite are the backbone of the green energy transition. As the primary component of Lithium-ion battery anodes, graphite demand is projected to quadruple by 2030.

Additionally, Magnetite remains the most important ore for high-grade steel, supporting the structural integrity of modern infrastructure.

Mineral Criticality Registry

Natural Graphite

High electrical conductivity and heat resistance. Essential for electric vehicle battery anodes.

Chemistry: C (Carbon)

Magnetite

Highly ferrimagnetic. A major iron ore used in industrial magnets and heavy-duty construction.

Chemistry: Fe3O4

Cassiterite

The primary ore of tin. Crucial for soldering in all electronic circuit boards.

Chemistry: SnO2
