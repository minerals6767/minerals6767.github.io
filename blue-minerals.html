The Cobalt Crunch

Blue minerals like Azurite and Cobaltite are at the heart of the "Blue Energy" revolution. Cobalt remains the critical component in high-density lithium-ion cathodes, providing the thermal stability required for rapid charging.

Furthermore, industrial-grade Sapphire is now the primary substrate for high-power LED manufacturing and scratch-resistant optical windows for military-grade drones.
