Luminescent Tech Analysis

Display Phosphors

Purple minerals like Sugilite and specific manganese-bearing silicates are critical in the development of next-generation OLED phosphors.

Beyond aesthetics, the Manganese (Mn) extracted from purple ores is a vital stabilizer in LMO Batteries (Lithium Manganese Oxide), providing higher thermal stability than standard cobalt-based cells.
